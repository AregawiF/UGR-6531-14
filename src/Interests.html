Other Interests

Maze Solving Micromouse

A maze-solving micromouse is a pint-sized marvel of robotics, designed to autonomously navigate intricate mazes. Outfitted with sensors like infrared or ultrasonic detectors, the micromouse skillfully maps its surroundings, strategically maneuvering through the labyrinth to reach a designated destination. This miniature robotic explorer exemplifies the convergence of programming, sensors, and algorithmic skills.

In competitions and educational settings, enthusiasts program the micromouse to efficiently traverse the maze, striking a balance between speed and accuracy. The challenge not only hones technical skills in robotics and software engineering but also fosters creativity in devising optimal paths. The micromouse phenomenon illuminates the excitement of hands-on learning, where tiny robots venture into complex mazes, translating code into dynamic, real-world navigation.

Internet Of Things (IoT)

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a revolutionary concept where everyday objects are connected to the internet, enabling them to collect and exchange data. From smart homes with connected devices to industrial applications, IoT transforms our world, enhancing efficiency and convenience. It's a dynamic network that empowers devices to communicate, offering unprecedented opportunities for automation, data analysis, and improved decision-making across various domains.
